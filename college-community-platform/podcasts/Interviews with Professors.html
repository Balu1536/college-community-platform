Episode Overview

In this episode, we bring you one-on-one conversations with some of KSRM College's most inspiring professors. They share their academic journeys, research interests, career advice, and how they connect with students beyond the classroom.

Featured Professors

  • Dr. N. Ramesh Babu (CSE): Discusses AI ethics, career tips for coders, and the future of programming.
  • Dr. M. Vijaya Lakshmi (ECE): Shares her passion for VLSI design and empowering women in engineering.
  • Dr. P. Suresh Kumar (Mechanical): Talks about industry-academia collaboration and innovation in manufacturing.
  • Dr. K. Sandhya Rani (Humanities): Explores student well-being, mental resilience, and communication skills.
