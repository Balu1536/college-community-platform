📣 KONNECT 2K25 & KSRM TROPHY 2K25

Dates: April 13th, 14th & 15th, 2025
Venue: KSRM College of Engineering, Kadapa

KONNECT 2K25 is an inter-college tech & cultural fest organized by KSRM College of Engineering. Along with KSRM Trophy 2K25 – the state-level sports tournament – the event promises to blend innovation, performance, and sportsmanship!

🧠 Technical Events – April 13, 2025 | 9:00 AM Onwards

  • CSE: Tech Quiz, Code Hunt, Web Design
  • ECE: Circuit Debugging, Poster Presentation
  • EEE: Paper Presentation, Quiz
  • MECH: Poster Design, Technical Treasure Hunt
  • CIVIL: Model Expo, Technical Quiz
  • S&H: Science Expo, JAM (Just a Minute)

🎭 Cultural Events – April 13 Afternoon

  • Solo Singing, Face Painting, Mehendi, Mimicry
  • Group Dance, Reels, Open Mic, Short Films
  • Skit, Fashion Show, One Minute Show, Photography

🏆 KSRM TROPHY 2K25 – April 9–10

For Boys: Cricket, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Chess, Carroms, Shuttle, Tug of War

For Girls: Tennikoit, Throwball, Tug of War, Carroms, Chess, Shuttle

🎪 Special Attractions

  • Food & Sponsor Stalls
  • QR-Based Registration
  • DJ Night, Fun Activities & Guest Talks
  • Exciting Prizes & Giveaways
