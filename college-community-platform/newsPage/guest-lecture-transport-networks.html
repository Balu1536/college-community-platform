Guest Lecture on “Research on Resilience of Transport Networks”

Resource Person: Dr. Chandra Balijepalli
PhD, FHEA, FCIHT, Associate Professor, Director of International Activities, Masters Admissions Tutor
Institute for Transport Studies, University of Leeds, UK

Lecture Topic: “Research on Resilience of Transport Networks”

Coordinator: Dr. I. Srinvasula Reddy, Assistant Professor, CE

Date: April 4th, 2025

Venue: Online & CE Seminar Hall
