Episode Overview

In this uplifting episode, we share inspiring journeys of students from different departments at KSRM College who have overcome challenges and achieved academic, personal, or professional milestones.

Featured Stories

  • Sai Kumar Reddy (CSE)- – From Kadapa to Infosys:Sai Kumar Reddy, a 2023 CSE graduate, started his journey with a passion for coding. During his time at KSRM, he actively participated in hackathons and coding competitions. With support from the Training & Placement Cell and his faculty mentors, he secured a placement at Infosys as a Systems Engineer. Today, he works on backend systems that support millions of users. Quote:

    "KSRM gave me the foundation and confidence I needed. The peer support, projects, and mock interviews made all the difference."

  • Lakshmi Priya – Championing Women in Mechanical Engineering: Lakshmi Priya broke barriers by excelling in Mechanical Engineering, a field traditionally underrepresented by women. She represented KSRM at national technical symposiums and completed her final-year internship at BHEL. She now works as a Design Engineer at a manufacturing unit in Hyderabad. Quote:

    "The workshops and hands-on lab sessions were turning points. I realized I was capable of achieving anything with hard work and the right support."

  • Rajesh Mandal – Startup Visionary:An Electronics and Communication graduate of the 2022 batch, Rajesh founded his own IoT-based startup focused on smart irrigation solutions for farmers. He credits his success to the entrepreneurship guidance he received at KSRM and the encouragement to participate in innovation summits. Quote:

    "The idea sparked during our final-year project. KSRM helped me polish the idea into a working prototype that is now solving real-world problems."

  • Haseena Parveen – Civil Engineering and Community Impact: Hailing from a small village in Andhra Pradesh, Haseena joined KSRM with dreams of improving rural infrastructure. After excelling in her academics and interning with the Kadapa Municipal Corporation, she joined an NGO as a Project Engineer, working on clean water and sanitation projects. Quote:

    "KSRM not only gave me technical skills but also taught me how to serve society using my education."
