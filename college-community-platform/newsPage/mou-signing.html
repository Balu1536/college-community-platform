🤝 MoU with EEE Energy Private Limited

Date: [Insert Date Here]
Venue: KSRM College of Engineering, Kadapa

K.S.R.M College of Engineering, Kadapa, has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with EEE Energy Private Limited, Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, to foster academic-industry collaboration.

📈 Partnership Goals:

  • Curriculum Enhancement: Improving academic content.
  • Internships: Providing real-world experience to students.
  • Research and Development: Collaborative projects with industry.
  • Faculty Training: Enhancing educators' skills.
  • Expert Lectures: Guest lectures by industry professionals.
  • Innovation Mentoring: Supporting student projects.
  • Employability Programs: Preparing students for the job market.
