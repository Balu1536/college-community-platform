🎨 Seminar on Career Development and Entrepreneurship in Interior Designing

Date: March 27, 2025
Time: 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM
Venue: CE Seminar Hall, KSRM College of Engineering, Kadapa

The Department of Civil Engineering is organizing an expert seminar on Career Development and Entrepreneurship in Interior Designing. This session is aimed at enlightening students about the prospects and growth paths in the field of interior design and the entrepreneurial opportunities it holds.

🗣️ Resource Person

  • Mr. Phanindra Reddy – Managing Director, Krowd Home Interiors

🎯 Coordinator

  • Sri. K. Hemanth Kumar Reddy – Assistant Professor, CE

✅ Organized By

Department of Civil Engineering, KSRMCE

All students interested in interior design and creative career paths are encouraged to attend!
